Gabriella Grill (b. 1993, Columbia, MD) is an interdisciplinary artist working primarily in sculpture. She studied Printmaking at the Maryland Institute of Art in Baltimore, Maryland, and received her BFA in 2015. She is currently a 2021 MFA candidate at Stanford University in Stanford, California. Grill has shown in Baltimore MD, Philadelphia PA, New York NY, Providence RI, and the Bay Area CA. She is a founding member of all of us, artist-run gallery and collective.
